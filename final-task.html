Indicator

7.b.1

Installed renewable energy-generating capacity in developing countries (in watts per capita)

 

This indicator measures the installed renewable energy-generating capacity in developing countries, measured in watts per capita. The goal is to increase renewable energy capacity and expand clean energy infrastructure in developing countries, especially in the least developed countries, small island developing states, and land-locked developing countries.

Learn More
Target 7.b
By 2030, expand infrastructure and upgrade technology for supplying modern and sustainable energy services for all in developing countries, in particular least developed countries, small island developing States, and land-locked developing countries, in accordance with their respective programmes of support

Indicator

7.a.1

International financial flows to developing countries in support of clean energy research and development and renewable energy production, including in hybrid systems

 

This indicator measures international financial flows provided to developing countries to support clean energy research, development, and renewable energy production. The goal is to increase investment in clean energy technology and infrastructure in developing countries.

Learn More
Target 7.a
By 2030, enhance international cooperation to facilitate access to clean energy research and technology, including renewable energy, energy efficiency and advanced and cleaner fossil-fuel technology, and promote investment in energy infrastructure and clean energy technology

Indicator

7.3.1

Energy intensity measured in terms of primary energy and GDP

 

This indicator measures energy intensity, which is the amount of energy used per unit of gross domestic product (GDP). The goal is to improve energy efficiency and reduce energy waste in economic activities.

Learn More
Target 7.3
By 2030, double the global rate of improvement in energy efficiency

Indicator

7.2.1

Renewable energy share in the total final energy consumption

 

This indicator measures the share of renewable energy in global final energy consumption. The goal is to increase the contribution of renewable energy in the global energy mix to reduce carbon emissions and other negative environmental impacts from fossil fuels.

Learn More
Target 7.2
By 2030, increase substantially the share of renewable energy in the global energy mix

Indicator

7.1.1

Proportion of population with access to electricity

 

This indicator measures the percentage of the population with access to electricity. The goal is to ensure that everyone, especially in rural areas and disadvantaged communities, has access to reliable and affordable energy sources.

Learn More

Indicator

7.1.2

Proportion of population with primary reliance on clean fuels and technology

 

This indicator measures the percentage of the population that relies on clean fuels and technologies as their primary source of energy. The goal is to reduce dependence on fossil fuels and increase the use of clean energy.

Learn More
Target 7.1
By 2030, ensure universal access to affordable, reliable and modern energy services
